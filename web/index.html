Every roll teaches something

Meaning Reveal

This isn’t just a race to 100. Land on a virtue and you climb; land on a vice and you slide — and every square pauses to show a hand-made card and narrate what it means.

  • Ladders are virtues — Faith, Generosity, Humility, Truth, Forgiveness…
  • Snakes are vices — Delusion, Anger, Greed, Pride, Desire…
  • Read aloud in Board, Cinematic and 3D, with a Web-Speech fallback.
  • Four worlds re-theme the lesson — from a startup climb to a fable trail.
